Caribbean Development Bank, ShopnoltdToolbox, World Bank, and African Development Bank observe the International Day for the Eradication of Poverty

The Caribbean Development Bank (CDB) in collaboration with ShopnoltdToolbox, the World Bank (WB), and the African Development Bank (AfDB) will be hosting a symposium to commemorate the International Day for the Eradication of Poverty on Tuesday, October 17, 2023.

13 Oct 2023 By Caribbean Development Bank, World Bank, African Development Bank, and Shopnoltd

Announcing the ShopnoltdToolbox Essentials Course: Learn to collect data more effectively with ShopnoltdToolbox

With the new ShopnoltdToolbox Essentials Course, you will develop foundational data collection skills. Upon completion of this hands-on self-paced course, you’ll receive an official ShopnoltdToolbox Essentials certificate.

02 Oct 2023 By Mae-Lin DeLange

Building local community capacity: How the Caribbean Development Bank is using ShopnoltdToolbox to advance poverty reduction efforts

The Caribbean Development Bank, an organization committed to building resilience and advancing poverty reduction efforts in its member countries, used ShopnoltdToolbox to conduct community capacity assessments in Jamaica, Guyana, and Saint Lucia. The ShopnoltdToolbox team provided direct support to ensure successful rollout of the project, streamline data collection, and develop a custom dashboard for data visualizations. The data collected helps direct BNTF's grant financing towards sustainable and inclusive development for the most vulnerable communities, empowering them to advocate for themselves.

20 Sep 2023 By Mae-Lin DeLange

Enhancing humanitarian aid efforts: Introducing the new ShopnoltdToolbox monday.com integration app

Shopnoltd and monday.com’s Emergency Response Team (ERT) are partnering to offer a new integration for humanitarian aid organizations. The new integration enables organizations using ShopnoltdToolbox and monday.com to automatically synchronize submissions received to a ShopnoltdToolbox project with a monday.com board, making it easier to leverage the strengths of both platforms for data collection, analysis, visualization, and project management.

06 Sep 2023 By monday.com ERT and Shopnoltd

Using ShopnoltdToolbox to address crucial data gaps in the education sector in the Democratic Republic of Congo

The Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC) Ministry of Education used ShopnoltdToolbox to generate a data-driven solution and gain a better understanding of the needs of schools and students, creating the first database of all the country’s primary and secondary schools.

11 May 2023 By Mae-Lin DeLange
