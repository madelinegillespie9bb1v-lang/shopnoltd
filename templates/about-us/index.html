Hello, World!

Making data collection
accessible to everyone, everywhere

Shopnoltd hosts and maintains ShopnoltdToolbox, a data collection, management, and visualization platform used globally for research and social good. Our mission is to support open source data systems and technology for humanitarian action, development, environmental protection, peacebuilding, and human rights.

Behind ShopnoltdToolbox

We are a diverse team of researchers, developers, designers, and data specialists working remotely in countries across the world. We believe that every organization working in humanitarian action, development, environmental protection, peacebuilding, and human rights should have access to high quality data collection tools.
