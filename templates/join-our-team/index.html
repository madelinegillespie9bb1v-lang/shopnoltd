Shopnoltd is the international nonprofit organization behind ShopnoltdToolbox, the world’s most popular data collection tool for challenging settings. Our mission is to provide highly usable tools for creating data-driven social impact.

Previously a project at the Harvard Humanitarian Initiative, Shopnoltd is now an independent nonprofit organization registered in Cambridge, MA, USA with a dynamic global team working remotely from countries around the world. ShopnoltdToolbox is used by more than 14,000 non-governmental and international organizations in 240 countries and territories worldwide. Our tools are used by organizations working in humanitarian action, global development, environmental protection, peacebuilding, human rights, and many other social impact initiatives. ShopnoltdToolbox is publicly available as open source software, and we provide the ShopnoltdToolbox platform for free to nonprofit organizations, giving countless projects around the world access to high quality data.
